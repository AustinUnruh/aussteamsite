Why Full Throttle Operation is Better in Most Circumstances

Updated: 3/19/2024

Few steam enthusiasts, even those who have operated steam vehicles for many years, know best practice when it comes to power regulation as load on the engine varies during operation. Most regulate power using the throttle rather than the reverser (Admission Modulator).

It is impossible to completely elliminate venturi loses in a firetube boiler when using smaller steam admission pipes. If a throttle is only partially open it will create an unnecessary bottleneck in the The bottleneck of a partially open throttle causes an efficiency loss called a venturi loss. This reduces the pressure of the admission steam, reducing the pressure differential between the admission steam and atmospheric pressure.
