Updated: 11/8/2024

Myth:

It is claimed by a select few sources that O. Winston Link played a pivotal role in saving Norfolk and Western 611

Truth:

There is no evidence to substantiate this claim, only Link's word. Thanks to Kenneth Miller of the Norfolk and Western Historical Society for providing credible information on this topic.
